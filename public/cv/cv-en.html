01 — ObjectiveProfessional Summary

With over 18 years of experience in mechanical installation and BIM, I have successfully managed technical office and site operations across diverse project types and scales — including shopping centres, airports, residential towers, and public buildings. I have taken an active role in establishing in-house BIM workflows, fully parametric 3D modelling of projects, and ensuring interdisciplinary coordination. I have extensive hands-on experience in model development from LOD 300 to LOD 450, clash detection, progress payment attachments, and As-Built deliverables. I am highly proficient in Revit MEP, Navisworks, AutoCAD, and SolidWorks. My goal is to further develop my expertise in BIM coordination and continue delivering sustainable, efficient solutions on large-scale projects.
